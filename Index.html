Gulfam Handicrafts Producer Company Ltd.

Gulfam Handicrafts Producer Company Ltd.

strives towards producing the finest Handicraft products by stepping on the ladder of aged old inherited skills and knowledge of Handicrafts. Our Company with its roots deeply buried in the prosperous Handloom and Handicraft sector of the J&K is endeavoring towards streamlining the unorganized skill possed by many into an organized one. The company also provides training to artisans and provides marketing services to help them sell their products. The company also provides online marketing and e-commerce services to help artisans sell their products online.
